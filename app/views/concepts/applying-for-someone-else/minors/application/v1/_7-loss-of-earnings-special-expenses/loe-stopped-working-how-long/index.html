Help understanding the timeframe

If the child has been unable to work or look for a job for 197 days or more (28 full weeks), they’ll be considered for loss of earnings and special expenses.

The 197 days can be a single period of time or cover several different times since the crime. For example, the child may return to work in the same week of the crime. But, they then cannot continue working due to the injuries they received during the crime.

We will ask you for more details about this when we get in touch after you’ve sent the application.
