Help with changes to the home

This applies to costs you or the child paid for changes within your home after the crime.

For example, you may have had to install the following in the home:

  • a stairlift
  • lifting devices
  • ramps for wheelchairs

An occupational therapist or similar professional must recommend or approve any changes. We may need to see receipts for these costs after you've sent your application.

It does not apply to the cost of buying a new home.
