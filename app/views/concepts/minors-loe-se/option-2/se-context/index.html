Applying for special expenses

The child may qualify for special expenses if they've experienced any of the following because of the injury:

  • lost money from their job as they were off
  • had to leave their job
  • were unable to work

This must be for a period of 6 months or more. It has to be at least 196 days (28 weeks) or more. This can be a single period of time or span several different periods of time.

Special expenses you and/or the child can apply for are considered as the following:

  • physical aids lost or damaged during the incident
  • cost of treatments delivered by the NHS
  • cost of treatments delivered by a state health service outside the United Kingdom
  • special equipment
  • required changes to the home as a result of the incident
  • cost of care that resulted from the incident
  • cost of supervision by care professionals
  • cost of administrators who handle their affairs
  • cost of setting up a trust as instructed by a claims officer

In applying for special expenses, we may ask you to provide evidence of any information you provide after you've sent the application.
