Applying for special expenses

In applying for special expenses, you confirm the child experienced one of the following for a period of six months or more because of the incident. They:

  • lost money from their job as they were off
  • had to leave their job
  • were unable to work

The period of six months or more must be equal to 28 weeks (196 days). This can be a single period of time or span several different periods of time.

Special expenses they can apply for include:

  • physical aids lost or damaged during the incident
  • cost of treatments delivered by the NHS
  • cost of treatments delivered by a state health service outside the United Kingdom
  • special equipment
  • required changes to the home as a result of the incident
  • cost of care that resulted from the incident
  • cost of supervision by care professionals
  • cost of administrators who handle their affairs
  • cost of setting up a trust as instructed by a claims officer
