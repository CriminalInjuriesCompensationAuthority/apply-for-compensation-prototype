The user is told upfront that the crime must have been reported to the police. This will reduce ineligible applications. You can tell the user to contact 101 to report it.

Advise users of the organisations that they can apply to if the crime did not happen in E/S/W or another relevant place, for example Northern Ireland, EUCAT.

Tell the user what information they need before they start as they cannot ‘save and return’.

Victims of sexual assault or abuse often apply outwith the 2 years. Reassure the user that we can make exceptions depending on their circumstances.

Direct users to the online service where possible, however, we should offer the telephone application service to support those with assisted digital needs.

Signpost the user to support services if you identify during the call that the user needs practical help or emotional support.
