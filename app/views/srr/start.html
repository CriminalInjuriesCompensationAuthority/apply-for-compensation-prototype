Heading - Enquire about compensation

Use this service to open an enquiry for compensation if you were the victim of a violent crime or abuse between August 1964 and October 1979 and were living under the same roof as the perpretrator. This includes if:

  • you were physically injured
  • you suffered mental injuries
  • were sexually assaulted or abused
  • you saw the crime happen to a family member (or were there immediately afterwards)
  • a family member died
  • you paid for the funeral of a family member
Open an enquiry

Before you start

There is a different service for Northern Ireland

You do not need a legal representative to open an enquire or subsequent application

Compensation is paid by the government, not the offender. We may be able to pay compensation even if the offender is not identified or convicted.

What you'll need

To complete the enquiry, you'll need:

  • Any previous application reference numbers
  • A telephone number

Eligibility

You must have been living under the same roof as the perpetrator at the time of the crime or abuse.

Other ways to apply

You can also enquire over the phone on 0300 003 3601. Select option 8.

Our phone lines are open Monday to Friday 8.30am to 5pm except Wednesday when they open at 10am.
