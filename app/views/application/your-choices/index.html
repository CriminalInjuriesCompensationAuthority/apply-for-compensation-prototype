What have you suffered?

Sexual assault or abuse

When you apply because of sexual assault or abuse, we can normally make a quicker decision based on information we get from the police.

If we pay you compensation, some of that money acknowledges the mental trauma you have suffered.

Sexual assault or abuse and other injuries or losses

We will need more evidence from you if you apply for compensation for:

  • other mental or physical injuries
  • loss of earnings
  • other expenses
  • pregnancy, loss of foetus or a sexually transmitted disease

It will normally take longer to make a decision in these cases.
