What happened to you?

We want to make applying for compensation as easy as possible for you. We pay compensation for sexual assault or abuse to recognise the distress and mental trauma you have suffered.

Sexual assault or abuse

We will:

  • ask the police to send us a report of the crime
  • not normally ask for your medical records
  • usually give you a decision within 3 months

Sexual assault or abuse and additional injuries or losses

As well as a police report, we:

  • will need information about your additional injuries or losses
  • will normally ask for your medical records
  • may arrange for an expert to diagnose your medical condition
  • will usually give you a decision within 12 months
