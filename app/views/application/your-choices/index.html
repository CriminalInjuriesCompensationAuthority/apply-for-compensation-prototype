What other injuries or other losses have you suffered?

We can make a fixed payment, based only on the police report, which contains an element that acknowledges the mental trauama you have suffered.

We would make our decision within 4 months but would not consider any other mental or physical injuries, loss of earnings, expenses or other consequences you suffered.

Disabling mental injuries

A disabling mental injury has a substantial adverse effect on your ability to carry out normal day to day activities. It does not include temporary mental anxiety, but may include:

  • impaired performance at work or school
  • negative effects on social relationships
  • negative impacts on your sex life and intimacy

Your disabling mental injury symptoms must have lasted for 6 weeks or more for us to consider paying compensation. We may also ask you to have your mental injuries assessed by a clinical psychologist.

Compensation for other losses

We can also consider applications for compensation for:

  • physical injuries
  • loss of earnings
  • other expenses for example, special equipment, providing care
  • other consequences of the crime for example, pregnancy, loss of foetus and sexually transmitted disease

Cases involving disabling mental and physical injuries and other losses are usually more complex and take more time for us to make a deicsion.
