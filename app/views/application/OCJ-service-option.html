There are two services you can use to apply for compensation for a sexual assult.

Service one

If you choose this option we'll:

  • ask the police to send us a report detailing the sexual assult
  • assess your application based on the information in the police report
  • assess your application based on the answers you give us

We won't be able to take account of any physical or mental injuries or any other costs.

If you use this service the maximum compensation award we can make is Â£20,000.

In most cases applications processed using this service take 6 weeks.

Service two

These applications are more complex and take longer to decide as we usually need additional information to make a decision.

If you choose this option we'll:

  • ask the police to send us a report detailing the sexual assult
  • ask you to get a medical report from your doctor
  • assess your application based on the information in the police report
  • assess your application based on the answers you give us
  • take account of any physical, mental injuries or any other costs

Use Service two
