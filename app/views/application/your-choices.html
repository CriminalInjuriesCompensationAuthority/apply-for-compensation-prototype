Choose an option

Your choice can affect how much information and evidence we will need to assess your application.

Option 1

We will get the police report, but you may need to:

  • provide medical reports about your physical injuries
  • have your mental injuries assessed by an expert
  • give us evidence of any loss of earnings or other expenses

We usually take around 11 months to make a decision.

Option 2

We will:

  • only need to get the police report
  • use the information in the report to make a decision
  • usually give you a decision within 16 weeks

This option will not consider any other injuries, loss of earnings, expenses or other consequences of the crime.
