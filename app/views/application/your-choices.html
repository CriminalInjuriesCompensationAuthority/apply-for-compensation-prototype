Your options

Your choice can affect how much information and evidence we need to assess your application.

Option 1

We will get information from the police.

Based on the facts of your case, you may need to:

  • get medical evidence of your physical injuries
  • have your mental injuries assessed by an expert
  • give us evidence of any loss of earnings or other expenses

We usually take around 11 months to make a decision.

Option 2

We will:

  • only need to get information from the police
  • use that information to make a decision
  • not consider any other injuries, loss of earnings, expenses or other consequences of the crime

We will usually give you a decision within 16 weeks.
