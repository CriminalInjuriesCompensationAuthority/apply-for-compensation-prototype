Victims of sexual assault: service options

We want to make your application for compensation as easy as possible. We pay compensation for crimes of sexual violence in recognition of the distress and mental trauma you've suffered. We can use fixed payments based on the nature of the assault to settle your application quickly and with minimal intrusion.

If you choose this service we'll:

  • check your eligibility
  • ask the police to send us the crime report
  • not need to obtain and examine your medical records
  • make a decision on your application within 8 weeks, provided there are no police investigations that prevent the report being released

If we can’t make a decision within 8 weeks we'll contact you to tell you why.

This service isn’t appropriate for you if:

  • you have additional physical injuries
  • you have suffered a disabling mental injury
  • you want to claim for loss of earnings or special expenses

These types of applications are more complex and take longer to decide as we need to examine your medical records or get a diagnosis from a psychiatrist or clinical psychologist
