Assessing your application

We'll need you to answer some more questions to assess your application fully. These questions will cover details of:

  • any physical injuries you've suffered
  • any mental injuries you've suffered
  • any loss of earnings or personal property

We may ask you to undergo a psychological assessment.

We'll ask you to provide us with evidence of any other losses of earning or personal property.

If you are eligible for compensation, we'll offer you a payment based on The Criminal Injuries Compensation Scheme.

Assessing your application like this can take a long time.
