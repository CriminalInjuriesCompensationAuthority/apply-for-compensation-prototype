Apply for criminal injuries compensation

Use this service to apply for compensation if you have been injured as a result of a violent crime in England, Wales or Scotland.

There is a separate service for Northern Ireland.

Start now

Before you start

To complete your application you'll need to know:

  • the date the crime happened
  • the crime reference number the police gave you when you reported the crime

You do not need to use a legal representative to help you with your application unless you want to. If you do use a representative, they may charge you a percentage of any money you get.

If you are a legal representative applying for compensation on behalf of a client you should use the Apply for compensation as a legal representative service.
