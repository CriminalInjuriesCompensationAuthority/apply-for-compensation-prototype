Before you start

To complete your application you will need to know:

  • dates the crime happened
  • name of the police force who recorded the crime
  • name and ID number of the police officer who recorded the crime
  • crime reference number the police gave you

You do not need to use a legal representative to help you with your application unless you want to. If you do use a representative, they may charge you a percentage of any money you get.
