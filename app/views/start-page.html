We recognise the distress and mental trauma victims (survivors) of sexual assault experience and offer two compensation claim options.

Simple assessment of your claim

A simple assessment uses only the details recorded in the police report to decide how much compensation you will get.

To complete a simple assessment we will not need to ask to see your medical records or other evidence.

We can usually complete this assessment in 8 weeks, if there are no problems getting the police report.

Detailed assessment of your claim

You should choose a detailed assessment if you want to claim additional compensation for:

  • physical injuries
  • disabling mental injuries
  • loss of earnings or special expenses

We will need to see more evidence such as medical records and it can take up to a year to make a decision, depending on how complicated the case is.

Before you start

You will need to have:

  • dates the crime happened
  • name of the police force who recorded the crime
  • name and ID number of the police officer who recorded the crime
  • crime reference number the police gave you
